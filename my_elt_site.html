Serious English Language Training

We are a group of experienced professionals that work with you and our frameworks that draw upon the best of foreign language learning methodology and instructional design to get you to your objective faster!

Situational Sessions

Have you ever missed out on an opportunity due to not speaking English?
We use the Context-Challenge-Activity-Feedback framework to make sure that never happens again!

Text-based Sessions

A text can be anything!
It can be:
An article you find interesting.
A Youtube video about your profession or hobby.
A classic movie that can give you insights into foreign culture.
A book you want to read in the original language.
That song that you were never quite sure you understood.

Test-Teach-Test Sessions

This session type is designed to:
Learn more vocabulary in a shorter timespan.
Isolate and train vocabulary that is crucial for your profession.

Integrated Tasks

The four main skills are:
Reading
Writing
Listening
and Speaking.
We design tasks that are relevant to your objectives that include all 4 main skills.

Project-Based Learning

We work with you to "kill two birds" with one stone!
We sign a non-dislosure agreement and can help you with your work.
We can help your write a presentation *and* practice presenting it!
